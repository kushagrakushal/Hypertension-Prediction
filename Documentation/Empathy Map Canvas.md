Empathy Map — Predictive Pulse (Hypertension Risk Patient)
User Persona: Adult aged 35–65 with unknown or unmonitored blood pressure

THINK & FEEL:
- "Am I at risk of a heart attack?"
- Worried about missing silent symptoms
- Hopes to avoid long-term medication
- Aspires to live healthily without fear
- Concerned about lifestyle changes needed

HEAR:
- Family: "Get your BP checked"
- Doctor: "Reduce salt intake"
- Media: "Hypertension is a silent killer"
- Friends: "I was fine until the crisis"

SEE:
- Friends/relatives with hypertension
- BP monitors at pharmacies
- Health apps on smartphones
- Doctors advising regular check-ups

SAY & DO:
- Delays doctor visits due to busy schedule
- Occasionally checks BP at pharmacy
- Searches symptoms on Google
- Says "I'll get a check-up soon"

PAIN:
- Fear of serious diagnosis
- No accessible early detection tool
- High cost of clinical consultations
- Confusion about BP readings

GAIN:
- Instant risk assessment from home
- Stage-based personalised advice
- Confidence to seek timely help
- Better understanding of own health
